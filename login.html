Battlechess Armada

Enter a display name to join the lobby list.

Battlechess Armada is a hidden-movement naval duel where each player commands a fleet on their own board, trying to sink the enemy King Flagship. You move one ship each turn, fire or take special actions, and use deduction to track your opponent's unseen fleet. It's chess-style strategy meets Battleship-style fog-of-war, with unique ship abilities, mines, shifting currents, and surprise interactions that keep every turn tense and tactical.
